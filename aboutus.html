    Pictures of Warung M'Bari Nyanyat

    Warung M’Bari Nyanyat began as a small business in 2021, operating from a modest mini stall at Pantai Kenangan, 89700 Bongawan, Sabah. With a vision to bring flavorful, home-cooked meals to the local community, it quickly gained the support from its customers. In 2022, after experiencing steady growth, Warung M’Bari Nyanyat moved to a larger, more permanent location which is their home yard at Kampung Silat, Jalan Pantai Kenangan, 89700 Bongawan, Sabah where they created a welcoming space that includes comfortable outdoor seating for their guests to enjoy their meals in a relaxed, fresh-air environment. Specializing in a variety of fried foods, they focus on delivering freshly prepared, delicious dishes that bring warmth and satisfaction with every bite. As a family-run business, they continue to prioritize quality, community, and the joy of sharing food with loved ones, staying true to our roots while expanding to serve even more customers.
